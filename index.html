Membership Pricing

When considering a fitness membership, it's important to understand the different pricing structures available.

Basic Membership

Our Basic Membership is perfect for those who want to take their first steps into fitness. Enjoy access to our state-of-the-art facilities and essential services.

$20 - $40

✓ Access to gym equipment(weight training equipment)

✓ Basic fitness classes

✓ Locker room and shower facilities

Standard Membership

Take your fitness journey to the next level with our Standard Membership. This plan offers more flexibility and additional perks to help you stay motivated and reach your goals.

$40 - $70

✓ Access to all gym equipment

✓ A wider range of fitness classes

✓ Some basic wellness services like steam rooms.

Premium Membership

Our Premium Membership offers all-inclusive access and exclusive benefits. Achieve your highest potential with personalized support and top-tier amenities.

$70 - $100

✓ Advanced fitness classes(All features of the Standard Membership)

✓ Personal training sessions

✓ Pool Access

BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation used to assess whether a person
has a healthy body weight for a given height.

BMI Calculator Chart

BMI Weight Status
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Healthy
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight
30.0 and above Obese
